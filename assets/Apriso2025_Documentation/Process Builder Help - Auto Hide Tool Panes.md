# Auto Hide Tool Panes

Activate Auto Hide on Tool Panes

To Activate Auto Hide on a Tool Pane

Use one of the following options:

           Click the Pin toggle button located in the upper-right corner of the tool pane
               to turn Auto Hide on
               to turn Auto Hide off

           Click the (Menu) drop-down menu in the upper-right corner of the tool pane and select
           Auto Hide from the drop-down.

When you activate Auto Hide, the pane contracts to the outside of the Entity Workspace shortly
after you move your mouse pointer away from it.

Hover over the tool pane name tab with your mouse to access it.
