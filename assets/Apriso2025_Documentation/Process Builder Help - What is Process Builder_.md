# What is Process Builder_

What is Process Builder?

DELMIA Apriso Process Builder is the primary Business Process Management tool and the core
application of DELMIA’s broader Apriso solution for manufacturing operations management.

As the modeling component of DELMIA Apriso, Process Builder allows for authoring user interfaces
and business logic with the use of Screen Flow Management design methodology and entities,
such as Projects, Screens, Layouts, Views, and DFCs (Functions). Process Builder capabilities
also ensure interaction with manufacturing operations modeling entities, such as Processes and
Operations, allowing for structuring and detailing components of the manufacturing operations. The
application enables quick and easy management and modification of the developed designs, giving
you the flexibility to change the business process as often as needed. Thanks to enhanced support
for reusability, previously developed logic can be quickly reused in new designs.

Deploying modifications within a specific plant or across multiple locations is then possible using
Global Process Manager capabilities. When deployed, other DELMIA Apriso modules can then be
used to perform activities such as interpret (Function Interpreter), integrate with machinery
(Machine Integrator), integrate with external systems and applications (Business Integrator and
Web Services), localize (Translation Tool), as well as provide the visibility for monitoring, analyzing,
and controlling the activities in real-time.

DELMIA Apriso Process Builder offers unprecedented ways to streamline operations management.
The benefits include:

           Reduced costs because of the Process Builder’s ease of use and lack of dependency on
           third-party development software.
           Modifications of business logic and user interface without customization or programming.
           Projects that let you control the delivery and maintenance of BPM-based solutions through
           grouping, versioning, and status control of all entities that belong to the solution.
           Support for global deployment across all areas of operations – production, warehouse,
           quality, maintenance, and extended supply chain.
           Built-in support for multiple languages and time zones.
