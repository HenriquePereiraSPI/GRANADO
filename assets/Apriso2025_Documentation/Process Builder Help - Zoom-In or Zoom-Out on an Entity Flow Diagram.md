# Zoom-In or Zoom-Out on an Entity Flow Diagram

Zoom In or Zoom Out on an Entity's Flow Diagram

Use one of the following options:

           Hold down the Ctrl key and zoom in and zoom out using the mouse scroll
           Click on the Zoom In and Zoom Out icons in the toolbar
           Right-click on the Zoom section of the Status Bar and use either:
           Pre-set zoom values (25% - 175%)
           Click Zoom In or Zoom Out to zoom in/out in 25% increments
